# DONNA Landing Page Mockup (v2)

## Design Intent
**Tone:** Impressive, confident, calm
**Vibe:** Dark Apple product page + Tesla-level inevitability
**Audience:** First-time SMB visitors who may dislike tech
**Primary Goal:** Make it obvious that DONNA is strategically designed AI infrastructure they could realistically use

---

## Global UI Direction
- Dark background throughout (near-black / deep indigo)
- Generous spacing, slow scroll rhythm
- Minimal visible UI chrome
- Subtle motion only (slow fades, gentle parallax, restrained glow)
- No feature grids above the fold
- No jargon, no hype language

---
