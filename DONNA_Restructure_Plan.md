# DONNA: Website Restructure Plan
**Target:** [aidonna.co](https://www.aidonna.co)  
**Status:** Ready for Antigravity Editing  
**Date:** May 2026

## 1. Narrative Alignment: The Reframe Mirror
**Section:** Reframe  
**Action:** Rewrite the "Reframe" section to act as a direct, structural mirror to the "Real Estate Reality" section. 

* **Logic:** For every specific pain point mentioned in the "Reality" section (e.g., leads sitting, communication breaking), the "Reframe" must provide the specific system-driven resolution.
* **Tone:** Maintain "Calm Intelligence." Avoid frantic sales language. Focus on the transition from "Fragmented Chaos" to "Structured Infrastructure."

## 2. Content Consolidation: Operational Infrastructure
**Action:** Merge the **"What DONNA actually does"** and **"AI harness"** sections. 
* **Reasoning:** These sections are conceptually identical. One explains the function, the other explains the "engine." 
* **New Narrative:** Combine them into one definitive block that explains DONNA as the system your business runs on. AI is the engine; DONNA is the vehicle/infrastructure.

## 3. Logical Continuity: Macro to Micro
**Action:** Re-order the vertical hierarchy of the middle-page sections.
1.  **Move the DIN (DONNA Intelligence Network)** above the "Real Estate Ecosystem" section.
2.  **The Result:** This establishes the "Network Effect" and compounding intelligence as a core platform feature *before* showing how that intelligence specifically connects Brokerages, Title, and Mortgage.

## 4. User Journey Refinement
**Action:** Relocate and Remove.
* **Relocate:** Move the **"Further Exploration"** section (NotebookLM and Investor Demo links) to sit directly under the **"Early Adopter Advantage"** section. This treats these links as "Research & Due Diligence" for high-intent leads.
* **Remove:** Delete the **"Real Estate Moves Fast"** CTA section. It is redundant and its high-pressure tone conflicts with the "Calm Intelligence" brand pillar.

## 5. Brand Fidelity Guidelines
All edits must strictly adhere to the established brand guide:
* **Branding:** DONNA is an **AI Operator** or **Infrastructure**. Never use "Chatbot," "VA," or "Assistant."
* **Visuals:** Abstract systems, neural nodes, and grids only. **No robots, faces, or humanoid visuals.**
* **Layout:** Prioritize generous padding and structured symmetry to reflect trust and power.
