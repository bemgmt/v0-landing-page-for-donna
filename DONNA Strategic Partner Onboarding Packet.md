DONNA Strategic Partner Onboarding Packet

Welcome to the DONNA Strategic Partner Program

We’re excited to welcome you as an early Strategic Partner for DONNA — the Digital Operations Neural Network Assistant.

DONNA is designed to act as an operational intelligence layer inside modern businesses, helping organizations streamline communication, workflows, automation, customer interaction, and decision flow across teams and systems.

As a Strategic Partner, your role is to introduce DONNA to businesses, organizations, and decision-makers who could benefit from operational AI infrastructure.

This packet outlines:

\- how the program works,  
\- who DONNA is for,  
\- how commissions are earned,  
\- how to position DONNA,  
\- and best practices for representing the brand.

Simple Positioning

\- automate repetitive work,

\---

Important Messaging

Good Phrases

\- “Operational AI platform”  
\- “AI business infrastructure”  
\- “AI-powered operational support”  
\- “Workflow coordination and automation”  
\- “Business communication intelligence”  
\- “Operational intelligence layer”

Avoid Saying

\- “Fully autonomous employee”  
\- “Guaranteed sales increase”  
\- “Replaces your entire staff”  
\- “Magic AI”  
\- “Unlimited automation”  
\- Unverified technical claims

Never promise features or integrations that have not been officially approved.
