DONNA Strategic Partner Program

Compensation Summary

What is DONNA?

DONNA (Digital Operations Neural Network Assistant) is an operational AI platform designed to help businesses manage communication, workflows, automation, customer interactions, and operational intelligence.

The Strategic Partner Program allows approved individuals and organizations to promote DONNA Early Adopter subscriptions and earn commissions on qualified signups.

\---

Commission Structure

DONNA Package| Customer Price| Partner Commission  
Early Adopter Starter| $500| $50  
Early Adopter Professional| $1,000| $100

Commissions are earned after:

\- customer payment is successfully processed,  
\- account is verified,  
\- and the transaction clears any refund/dispute period.

\---

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee and Commission Chargebacks

\- All Early Adopter subscriptions include a **30-day money-back guarantee** for the customer.  
\- If a customer receives a full refund under this guarantee within the 30-day window, **commissions on that sale may be charged back, reversed, or withheld** to reflect the returned revenue.  
\- Chargebacks, disputes, or fraudulent activity may also result in commission reversals, as described below.

\---

Weekly Payouts

\- Commission payouts are issued every Friday  
\- Payments may be sent via ACH, PayPal, Stripe, or Zelle  
\- Refunds, chargebacks, or fraudulent activity may result in commission reversals

\---

Optional Partner Pricing

Strategic Partners are not required to purchase DONNA.

However, approved Partners may access discounted pricing for their own business use:

Package| Partner Discount  
$500 Package| 10% Off  
$1,000 Package| 25% Off

Discounted subscriptions are non-transferable and may not be resold without authorization.
