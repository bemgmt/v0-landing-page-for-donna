# AI Prompt Cheat Sheet

A compilation of business prompts from the AI Business Workshop. For best results, upload your AI Employee Handbook files (`me.md`, `brandguidelines.md`, `rules.md`, `companyknowledge.md`) before running these prompts.

---

## 1. Marketing Prompts

### Social Media Post
```text
Act as my marketing manager. Using the information in my me.md, brandguidelines.md, and companyknowledge.md files, create a Facebook post promoting my services.
```

### 30-Day Content Calendar
```text
Create a 30-day content calendar for my business including:
- Educational posts
- Promotional posts
- FAQs
- Customer stories
- Community engagement
```

### SEO Blog Article
```text
Write a 1,000-word SEO blog article targeting [KEYWORD] based on our brand voice guidelines and company knowledge.
```

---

## 2. Sales Prompts

### Follow-Up Email
```text
Write a warm but professional follow-up email to a prospect who has not responded to our proposal for two weeks. Keep it under 150 words and include a single clear CTA.
```

### Objection Handling
```text
A customer said: "It costs too much."
Using our companyknowledge.md details, provide:
- Likely underlying concerns
- Suggested empathetic responses
- Relevant follow-up questions
```

### Proposal Draft
```text
Create a professional proposal outline for [CLIENT NAME] including:
- Project Scope
- Timeline
- Expected Benefits
- Pricing Summary (referencing our companyknowledge.md)
- Next Steps Call-to-Action
```

---

## 3. Customer Service Prompts

### FAQ Generator
```text
Generate the 25 most common customer questions and answers for our business based on the services and FAQs in companyknowledge.md.
```

### Customer Response
```text
Respond to the following customer message using my brand voice and rules:
"[INSERT CUSTOMER MESSAGE HERE]"
```

---

## 4. Operations Prompts

### Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)
```text
Create a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for [PROCESS NAME]. Include:
- Purpose
- Step-by-step instructions
- Common mistakes to avoid
- A final completion checklist
```

### Meeting Notes Summary
```text
Convert the following meeting notes into structured action items. Identify:
- Decisions made
- Action items
- Owners for each task
- Deadlines
---
[INSERT MEETING NOTES HERE]
```
