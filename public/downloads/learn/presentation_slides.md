## Slide 2: The Core Problem
# Why AI Gives Generic Answers
- AI doesn't know who you are
- AI doesn't know your business or products
- AI doesn't know your tone or customer style
- **Result:** You get generic, boring copy that sounds like everyone else.

---

## Slide 3: The Core Shift
# Prompting vs. Training
- **Prompting:** Telling AI *what* to do.
- **Training:** Telling AI *who you are* and *how* you do it.
- **Core Concept:** "Most people think they're learning how to prompt. What they're really learning is how to train AI to understand their business."

---

## Slide 4: The AI Employee Handbook
# The Four Core Files
We create 4 simple `.md` (Markdown) files:
1. **me.md** - Who you are and what you do.
2. **brandguidelines.md** - How you communicate.
3. **rules.md** - Quality standards and guardrails.
4. **companyknowledge.md** - Products, pricing, and FAQs.

---

## Slide 5: How It Works
# The Context Upload Workflow
1. Start a new chat session.
2. Upload your four handbook files.
3. Give your prompt (e.g., "Write a newsletter...").
4. AI now writes with precise context, tone, and accuracy.

---

## Slide 6: Best Practices
# Keep Files Up to Date
- Update `companyknowledge.md` when pricing or products change.
- Refine `rules.md` when you find mistakes the AI makes.
- Treat these files like living employee training manuals.
