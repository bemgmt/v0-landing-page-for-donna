DONNA Approved Messaging & Brand Communication Guide

Strategic Partner Messaging Standards

\---

Purpose of This Guide

This document outlines approved positioning, messaging, terminology, and communication standards for Strategic Partners representing DONNA.

The goal is to:

\- maintain brand consistency,  
\- protect the DONNA reputation,  
\- prevent inaccurate claims,  
\- and ensure professional communication with prospects and customers.

All Strategic Partners are expected to follow these standards when discussing DONNA publicly or privately.

\---

Core DONNA Positioning

Primary Positioning Statement

DONNA is an operational AI platform designed to help businesses coordinate communication, workflows, automation, customer interaction, and operational intelligence across teams and systems.

Approved Terminology

Preferred Language

Use:

\- operational intelligence  
\- workflow coordination  
\- AI-powered support  
\- operational infrastructure  
\- business communication management  
\- AI-assisted operations  
\- operational efficiency  
\- centralized intelligence  
\- communication workflows

\---

Terms to Avoid

Avoid:

\- “AI employee replacement”  
\- “Fully autonomous staff”  
\- “Unlimited automation”  
\- “Magic AI”  
\- “100% automated business”  
\- “Guaranteed revenue increase”  
\- “Guaranteed labor reduction”  
\- “Human-free operations”  
\- “Perfect AI”  
\- “Artificial employee”  
\- “Sentient AI”

\---

Prohibited Claims

Strategic Partners may NOT claim that DONNA:

\- guarantees increased revenue,  
\- guarantees cost reduction,  
\- replaces entire teams,  
\- provides legal advice,  
\- provides financial advice,  
\- operates fully autonomously,  
\- eliminates the need for staff,  
\- has integrations not officially announced,  
\- has certifications not officially documented,  
\- can perform actions outside approved functionality.

\---

Messaging Safety Rules

Never Promise

\- guaranteed ROI,  
\- guaranteed automation outcomes,  
\- guaranteed sales increases,  
\- guaranteed staffing reductions,  
\- or future features that are not officially released.

\---

AI Transparency Standard

If asked whether DONNA uses AI, approved language includes:

“DONNA uses AI-assisted operational workflows and communication support designed to help businesses improve organization and efficiency.”

Avoid exaggerated or misleading statements about AI capabilities.

Approved Sales Approach

The preferred DONNA sales approach is:

\- consultative,  
\- conversational,  
\- operationally focused,  
\- and relationship-driven.

Focus on:

\- identifying pain points,  
\- understanding workflows,  
\- operational bottlenecks,  
\- communication issues,  
\- and growth challenges.

DONNA should feel like:

«operational support infrastructure,  
not a gimmick.»

\---

Approved Prospect Conversation Starters

General

“How is your team currently managing communication and workflow coordination?”

“What operational tasks consume the most time each week?”

“Where do things usually bottleneck operationally?”

\---

Growth-Oriented

“Are your current systems keeping up with your business growth?”

“Where do you feel operational strain the most right now?”

\---

AI-Oriented

“Have you started exploring AI tools for operations or workflow support yet?”

“What would you ideally want AI to help your business handle?”

\---

Approved Responses to Common Questions

“Is DONNA a chatbot?”

“DONNA includes communication capabilities, but it is positioned more broadly as an operational AI platform for business coordination and workflow support.”

\---

“Does DONNA replace employees?”

“DONNA is designed to support operations and improve efficiency, not replace human decision-making or relationships.”

\---

“Can DONNA fully automate my business?”

“DONNA is intended to assist operational workflows and communication, while businesses maintain human oversight and operational control.”

\---

Social Media Guidelines

Strategic Partners may:

\- share approved DONNA marketing materials,  
\- discuss operational AI concepts,  
\- promote approved pricing/programs,  
\- and direct prospects toward official DONNA channels.

Strategic Partners may NOT:

\- create misleading advertisements,  
\- impersonate DONNA leadership,  
\- modify branding without approval,  
\- or publish false technical claims.

\---

Escalation Rules

Strategic Partners should escalate conversations involving:

\- enterprise contracts,  
\- white-label opportunities,  
\- advanced integrations,  
\- legal/compliance questions,  
\- technical architecture,  
\- or custom operational deployments.

\---

Final Messaging Principle

DONNA should always be positioned as:

«operational intelligence infrastructure that helps businesses coordinate communication, workflows, automation, and operational support more effectively.»

Not:

«hype AI promising unrealistic autonomy.»

Protecting trust, clarity, and long-term reputation is critical to the DONNA brand.
