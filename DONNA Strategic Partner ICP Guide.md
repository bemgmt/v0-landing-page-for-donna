DONNA Strategic Partner ICP Guide

Ideal Customer Profile & Qualification Framework

\---

What is an ICP?

ICP stands for Ideal Customer Profile.

This guide helps Strategic Partners identify:

\- the types of businesses DONNA is best suited for,  
\- who is most likely to buy,  
\- how to qualify opportunities,  
\- and where DONNA delivers the strongest operational value.

The goal is to focus on businesses where DONNA can create immediate operational impact.

\---

Core DONNA Positioning

DONNA is an operational AI platform designed to help businesses:

\- coordinate communication,  
\- streamline workflows,  
\- improve responsiveness,  
\- organize operational knowledge,  
\- automate repetitive tasks,  
\- and centralize operational intelligence.

DONNA is best positioned as:

«operational infrastructure for modern businesses.»

Signs a Prospect is a Strong Fit

Green Flags

\- “We’re overwhelmed.”  
\- “Things fall through the cracks.”  
\- “We need better organization.”  
\- “We respond too slowly.”  
\- “We need automation.”  
\- “Our staff is stretched thin.”  
\- “We need better systems.”  
\- “We’re growing too fast.”  
\- “Our communication is messy.”

These are strong DONNA indicators.

\---

Signs a Prospect May NOT Be a Good Fit

Red Flags

\- Looking for fully autonomous replacement staff  
\- Extremely small/non-operational businesses  
\- No digital communication systems  
\- No operational complexity  
\- No willingness to adopt technology  
\- Unrealistic AI expectations  
\- Seeking “magic button” automation

\---

DONNA Qualification Questions

Use these conversationally.

Operational Questions

\- How does your team currently manage communication?  
\- What operational tasks take the most time?  
\- Where do delays or bottlenecks happen most often?  
\- How are leads or customer requests handled today?  
\- What systems feel disorganized?

\---

Scaling Questions

\- Are you planning to grow this year?  
\- Do you expect increased customer volume?  
\- Are you hiring or expanding locations?  
\- What operational challenges come with growth?

\---

AI Readiness Questions

\- Have you used AI tools before?  
\- Are you exploring automation right now?  
\- What would you want AI to help with first?  
\- Where do you think your business loses the most time?

Strategic Partner Focus

The goal is not simply:

«“Who can buy AI?”»

The goal is:

«“Which businesses experience enough operational friction that DONNA becomes valuable infrastructure?”»

That distinction is important.

\---

Best Partner Mindset

Strong DONNA partners:

\- listen before pitching,  
\- identify operational pain points,  
\- position DONNA strategically,  
\- and focus on long-term relationships.

DONNA performs best when introduced as:

\- operational support,  
\- coordination infrastructure,  
\- and business intelligence assistance.

Not as:

\- hype AI,  
\- gimmick automation,  
\- or unrealistic “replace your team” technology.

\---

Final Qualification Rule

The best DONNA prospects usually have:

1\. Operational complexity  
2\. Communication volume  
3\. Growth pressure  
4\. Organizational friction  
5\. Desire for modernization

When those five conditions exist together, DONNA becomes highly valuable.
