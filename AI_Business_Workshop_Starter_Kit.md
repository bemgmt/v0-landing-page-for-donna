# AI Business Workshop Starter Kit

## Workshop Goal
Teach business owners how to use AI effectively by providing context through Markdown (.md) files.

Core concept:
> Most people think they're learning how to prompt AI. What they're really learning is how to train AI to understand their business.

---

# Class Outline

## Part 1: What is AI?
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Perplexity
- NotebookLM

### Simple Explanation
LLMs are prediction engines. They do not know your business unless you provide context.

---

## Part 2: Why AI Gives Generic Answers

AI doesn't know:
- Who you are
- What your business does
- Your customers
- Your products
- Your communication style
- Your business rules

Context improves results dramatically.

---

# The AI Employee Handbook

## 1. me.md

Purpose:
Teach AI who you are.

Suggested Sections:
- Name
- Company
- Role
- Location
- Background
- Goals
- Skills
- Communication Style
- Preferred Tone

---

## 2. brandguidelines.md

Purpose:
Teach AI how your company communicates.

Suggested Sections:
- Company Overview
- Elevator Pitch
- Brand Voice
- Audience
- Preferred Messaging
- Words/Phrases to Avoid
- Writing Style
- Marketing Style

---

## 3. rules.md

Purpose:
Teach AI your standards.

Suggested Sections:
- General Rules
- Content Rules
- Marketing Rules
- Email Rules
- Customer Service Rules
- AI Rules

Examples:
- Never invent facts
- Ask questions when information is missing
- Prioritize accuracy
- Keep explanations simple

---

## 4. companyknowledge.md

Purpose:
Teach AI what your business knows.

Suggested Sections:
- Products
- Services
- Pricing
- FAQs
- Team Members
- Processes
- Contact Information
- Locations
